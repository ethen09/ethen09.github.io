Home

Skateboarding is a sport and recreation, which a person rides on a board balanced on two sets of wheels. This hobby is popular with the younger crowd, most likley due to it's reputation as an 'extreme' sport as well as it's proximity to progressive cultures and self expression. To me skateboarding is an oppurtunity for diversity in community and companionship.

Starting small in the 1950s as surfboards with wheels, skateboarding has evolved into a cultural phenomenon. It's not just about the adrenaline or the rebellious nature, it's about the community, the art, and the freedom and represention. Today skateboarding has grown into a culture and lifestyle, many people making their own out of it, a way to escape their day to day lives.

About

I started skateboarding when I was younger, in middleschool. I did it to get outside, I didn't have convenient friends to play with. Once I started skateboarding, I'd run into people who also skateboard and with that I began to feel a sense of community. That was enough to keep me hooked, then along with it came the culture which I resonated with. I no longer skate for the fun of it but the community and people keep me here, even if I can't do all the coolest tricks I know I had lots of fun hanging out with friends and making memories. Contact me below and maybe we could hang out and skate sometime.

Schedule

The best times for skating

Season Description
Winter In the afternoon when the sun is the brightest. The warm sun and the brisk air will keep you cool as you skate and run tricks over and over, .
Spring Early in the morning or just in the afternoon before the tempurature reaches it's peak. The spring is allergy season but if you can manage this could be the most magical time of year to skate, plants blooming and its the first time alot of pople go outside that year.
Summer Early in the morning or late at night is the trick. Skating in the summer can be tricky depending on where you live. The sun can either be a blessing or a curse, dehydrating you and blistering your skin or glowing to inspire you to keep trying that line your friend challenged you to.
Fall In the afternoon or peak of the evening. This is my personal favorite time to skate. I love the fall for it's cool air. It's just cool enough to wear a jacket and maybe take it off as you get warmed up.

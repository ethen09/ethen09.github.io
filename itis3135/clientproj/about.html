Our Mission

We seek to create an inclusive enviorment for students to explore their hobbies, develop skils, and build relationships through collaborative events, competitive play and appreciating niche interests we rarley see outside of the interent.

What do we do?

  • Game nights (video, board, and card games)
  • Tournaments with prizes
  • Game Showcases
  • Game jams and Hackathons
  • Social/Networking events
  • Beginner-friendly workshops
  • Mentor workshops

Club Rules

  1. Respect all players and equipment.
  2. Be a good sport.
  3. No bullying or trash-talking.
  4. School policies apply (no inappropriate content).
  5. No Gatekeeping.
  6. Never Give up.

FAQ

Can I participate without joining the club?

Yes! Participation is welcome but unless you are a member you have limited access within the club. Members can vote, organize and discuss events. Become a member today! Join!

What are the requirements to join?

A student ID and your passion.

Do I need experience to join?

No! All skill levels are welcome. Just be a good sport.

What games do you play?

Video games, board games, card games you name it! You can even submit suggestions in our events tab.
